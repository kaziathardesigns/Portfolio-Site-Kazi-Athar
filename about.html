About

My Story

Hi! I am Kazi, a UI/UX Designer with a background in Environmental Science and sustainability. Based in Helsinki, I'm available for local and international projects and willing to relocate. Having learned early on the value of sustainable design, I apply my natural empathy and awareness of others to create user-centric solutions to design challenges. I also possess solid research skills, and a goal-oriented working style. I like to start any project with thorough research, once I have that in check, I begin jotting down ideas to design and test with users. To see more of my design process, check out my work samples, or reach out to me for a chat! Looking forward to hearing from you!
